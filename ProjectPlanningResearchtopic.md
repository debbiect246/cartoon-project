## How Cartoons Influence Toy Sales
[From Screen to Self investigating the influence of cartoons in behaviour of toys and accesories market](https://www.academia.edu/123346173/From_Screen_to_Shelf_Investigating_the_Influence_of_Cartoon_Characters_on_Consumer_Behavior_in_the_Toys_and_Accessories_Market)

Highlights 

- Licenced Cartoon Characters influenced Customers behaviour in the Toy and Accessories Market .
- 72% of parents by toys which are their child’s favourite Characters .
- Licenced characters that involved characters targeted to children generated $113.2 billion in sales in the US.
- Children that had an emotional connection to the character increase Brand Loyalty and  and recognition on consumers.
- Disney’s Frozen  exemplified the impact of character relatability and purchasing decisions.
- More children will want to have food with  cartoons characters such as the Minions and Hello Kitty on the box then real life Celebrities (Journal of Marketing Management and Consumer Behaviour, 2018)
- Younger children will be influenced in branding form licenced characters while older children will have their own personal style which was influenced from when they were younger
